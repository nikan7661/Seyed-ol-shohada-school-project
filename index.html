Welcome to Tehran

Tehran, the vibrant capital of Iran, blends tradition with modernity. From its bustling bazaars to its towering skyscrapers, every street has a story to tell.

Culture

Tehran is the cultural heartbeat of Iran, home to numerous museums, art galleries, and theaters. Persian traditions live on through music, poetry, and celebrations.

Food

From mouth-watering kebabs to saffron-infused stews, Tehran offers a paradise for food lovers. Don’t miss the chance to try street food at Tajrish Bazaar.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit: Spring and Autumn
  • Always carry cash for smaller shops
  • Public transport is efficient and cheap
